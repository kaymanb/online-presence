I am currently an MSc student in the Theory Group at the University of Toronto, where I am fortunate to be supervised by Professor Faith Ellen. My research interests are in computability, logic, complexity theory, and the theory of distributed computing.

Before this, I completed my undergraduate degree in mathematics and computer science, also at the University of Toronto. I also worked as a software engineer at Flipp and RBC Capital Markets.

I am passionate about hiking, canoeing, cycling, and anything related to the great outdoors. You can find photos from some of my trips on Instagram. I am also a fan of literary fiction, and I keep an infrequently-updated list of books that I've enjoyed.
