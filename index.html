Resume

I received my MSc from the Department of Computer Science at the University of Toronto, where I was part of the Theory Group and was very fortunate to be supervised by Professor Faith Ellen. My research interests were in the complexity theory and the theory of distributed computing.

Before that, I completed my undergraduate degree in mathematics and computer science, also at the University of Toronto. I have also worked as a software engineer at Flipp and RBC Capital Markets.

I am passionate about long-distance hiking, canoeing, running, bicycling, and anything else related to the great outdoors. You can find photos from some of my trips on Instagram. I am also a fan of literary fiction, and I keep an infrequently-updated list of books that I've enjoyed.
